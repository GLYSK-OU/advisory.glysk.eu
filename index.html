GLYSK Advisory is a technology advisory boutique specialising in business transformation for EU-based companies. We work where complexity lives — tech maturity assessment, use case definition, proof of concept, feasibility analysis, ROI modelling, and change management.

We developed a proprietary framework and methodology built around one starting point: the Inflection Scan — a focused two-week exploration designed to surface criticalities, failure points, change management exposure, and transformation readiness. From there we build the AI foundation model for the company. Only then do we support implementation: software delivery, process automation, RPA application and switching, and full AI adoption. Every decision is data-driven. KPI evidence in hand, always.

Where we engage with early-stage companies or startups, we may co-invest directly through GLYSK Holdings OÜ — formerly The Grid Capital. This is a residual activity, not our primary focus. Advisory leads. Everything else follows.
